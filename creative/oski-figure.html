Oski Figure

As you can probably tell from my portfolio, I have a bit of an obsession with my school’s mascot, Oski. They didn’t sell any Oski figures at the merch store, so I figured… why not make my own? I started with a quick rough sketch in Ibis Paint, then modeled the figure in Nomad Sculpt. After that, I 3D printed it on my Bambu Lab A1, painted it, and now I’ve got my dream desk accessory. Definitely one of my favorite little projects!
