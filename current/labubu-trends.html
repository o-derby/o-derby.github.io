Predictive Study on POPMART's Labubus

COMING SOON: This project will explore the rise of Labubu collectibles, comparing current hype to previous collectible trends. My plan is to gather data from social media, resale prices, and community buzz to see what factors drive popularity and how long these spikes last. I want to apply predictive modeling techniques to guess where the market might go next. It’s still early, but I’m excited to dig into this blend of cultural trends and data science. I just got a Labubu a few weeks ago so hopefully my model shows the trend won't die out too soon!
