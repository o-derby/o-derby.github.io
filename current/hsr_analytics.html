Honkai Star Rail (HSR) Marketing Strategy Study

COMING SOON: This project focuses on drip marketing in Honkai: Star Rail, specifically, how early character reveals and teaser content affect public engagement. I’m planning to analyze post and comment activity from platforms like Reddit and X around each campaign to see how hype builds over time and what types of "drips" generate the most attention. The idea is to connect marketing technique to community reaction, using data to better understand how staggered content release can shape excitement. I know for a fact their strategy works on me because as soon as a character is leaked, I immediately start to save for them!
